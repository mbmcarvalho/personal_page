Projects

The northerly winds in Aveiro: analysis of its behaviour in contemporary climate and future climate scenarios

The study involved the analysis of northerly winds in the Aveiro region, using historical data (1995-2014) and climate projections from the CMIP6 project for future scenarios (SSP2-4.5, SSP3-7.0 and SSP5-8.5). The focus of the project was to investigate how wind behaviour might change under different climatic conditions.
